## Creating an Apple ID (If Needed)

### Step 1: Create Apple ID (Free)
1. Go to: https://appleid.apple.com/account
2. Click "Create Your Apple ID"
3. Fill in:
   - Name
   - Email (or create @icloud.com email)
   - Password
   - Security questions
4. Verify your email
5. Done! ‚úÖ

**This is FREE and takes 5 minutes.**
